## Summary

scale≡ row column is 0.00 for all 53 matrix-heavy tensors. Even by
relaxed thresholds (rel 1e-3, rel 1e-2) the values stay near zero —
the deployed s_g is *consistently different* from mean(|w_base|_g),
not noise around it. So the recipe explicitly chose new scales rather
than carrying base's group means forward.

embed_tokens gives byte-equality 99.94% — Bonsai's embed at 8B is the
deterministic formula applied to Qwen3-8B-base.embed.

lm_head sits in between: 89.92% sign-match (so most signs come from
Qwen3-base) but 0.16% scale-match — its scales were modified, just
its signs largely preserved.

v_proj consistently has the highest sign-preservation (~80%) among
matrix-heavy projections; mlp.up the lowest (62-75%).

Layer 1-3 MLP has notably lower sign-match (62-69%) than layers 4-7
MLP (75-83%). Pattern: early MLP got pushed harder than mid MLP for
the layers we have on disk.
