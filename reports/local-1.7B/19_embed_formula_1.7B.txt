## Reading

The byte-match at abs 1e-3 is 99.93% — same as 8B's 99.94%. Sign
agreement is 99.93% — same as 8B's 99.94%. The strict scale-match
percentage (rel 1e-4) is 4.1% (vs 8B's 3.4%) — both small for the
strict threshold but byte-match shows the values are still in noise.

So: **embed = formula(Qwen3-base.embed) is a size-invariant property
at the sizes we've checked**. Whatever pipeline step produces the
matrix-heavy weights, the embed gets the deterministic-formula
treatment at both 1.7B and 8B (and presumably 4B).

Cross-check on L0 q_proj at 1.7B: sign agreement 0.6984, scale
match (rel 1e-4) 0.000000. Replicates the matrix-heavy pattern from
8B (~75% sign agreement, 0% scale match). The size-invariant
matrix-heavy fingerprint also holds.

## Recipe constraint (cross-size confirmed)

A reproduction at any size should compute the embedding tensor by
the deterministic formula on the teacher's embedding and never
touch it again. This is now confirmed at 1.7B and 8B; expected to
hold at 4B and other sizes by the same logic.

The 99.93% byte-match (not 100%) likely reflects FP16 round-trip
noise plus the trim-row alignment — informative, not contradictory.
